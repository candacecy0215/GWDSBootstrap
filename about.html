About WOK with You

Introducing Mindy and Mike's groundbreaking initiative: a restaurant and café like no other. Welcome to a culinary haven where sustainability meets compassion, and every meal tells a story of resilience and community empowerment.

Nestled in the heart of our city, our "Pay As You Feel" restaurant and café is a beacon of hope in the fight against food waste. Led by newlyweds Mindy and Mike, who bring with them a legacy of sustainable farming, we're on a mission to transform the way we think about food.

Here, every dish is a testament to our commitment to diverting fresh food from the grasp of landfills. Our daily changing menu is a canvas of creativity, showcasing the vibrant flavors of rescued produce and foodstuffs that would have otherwise gone to waste.

But our vision extends far beyond the confines of our kitchen. We believe that everyone deserves access to nourishing food, regardless of their financial circumstances. That's why we offer a "Pay As You Feel" Fresh Food Box subscription, brimming with rescued treasures from local supermarkets and farmers' markets, available for collection at select pick-up locations across the city.

At our restaurant, patrons aren't just customers; they're partners in our mission. Whether you choose to contribute financially, volunteer your time, or simply share in the joy of a delicious meal, every act of support helps us make a difference.

Join us as we redefine the meaning of food waste and foster a community of compassion and sustainability, one plate at a time. Welcome to a dining experience where generosity knows no bounds and every bite is a celebration of abundance and unity. Welcome to our restaurant!
